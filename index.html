About Me

I'm Jeremy Bryan Villanueva, a passionate video editor with a flair for storytelling through visuals. With over 10 years of experience in the video production industry, I’ve had the privilege of working on a wide variety of projects ranging from cinematic films to corporate videos, social media campaigns, YouTube content, and commercial advertisements. I’ve collaborated with brands, creators, and organizations to bring their ideas to life through high-quality editing, compelling narratives, and impactful visuals.

My expertise lies in creating immersive stories by combining technical skills with creativity. Whether it's through seamless cuts, dynamic transitions, color grading, motion graphics, or sound design, my focus is always on ensuring that every frame serves the story. I have a strong belief that every project, no matter the scale, should communicate a unique vision, evoke emotions, and engage audiences.

I specialize in using industry-leading software such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve, and After Effects. These tools are my second nature, and I continuously strive to improve my skill set by experimenting with new editing techniques, visual effects, and storytelling methods. I am also well-versed in audio mixing and sound design, ensuring that the soundscapes of my projects match their visual tone.

Throughout my career, I have worked with clients across a wide range of industries, including technology, entertainment, lifestyle, and education. I've had the pleasure of working on content for brands like NovaTech Solutions, YouTube influencers, and independent artists. Whether it's creating a cinematic promotional video for a tech startup or editing an engaging vlog for a travel influencer, I bring my creative vision to every project.

As a constant learner, I’m always looking to evolve in the ever-changing world of video editing. Staying up-to-date with the latest software updates, editing techniques, and industry trends allows me to push the boundaries of what’s possible. Collaboration is at the heart of my process, and I thrive when working alongside talented teams to create something memorable.

My goal is to continue telling powerful stories that inspire, entertain, and inform. Whether you're looking to create an impactful film, a brand video, or captivating social media content, I’d love to collaborate and bring your ideas to life.

Let's create something unforgettable together!

Skills & Work Experience

Video Editing

I am an expert in video editing, with years of experience working across various types of projects, including corporate videos, music videos, vlogs, and feature films. I am proficient in trimming, cutting, and sequencing footage to create a cohesive story. I have a strong understanding of pacing, rhythm, and flow, which are essential to crafting engaging and dynamic edits. I also specialize in synchronizing audio and visuals, ensuring that each frame supports the overall narrative and emotional tone of the video.

Software Expertise: Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve.

Color Grading

Color grading is a crucial part of my editing process. I’m skilled in color correction and grading, helping to enhance the mood, tone, and visual impact of each scene. Using DaVinci Resolve and Premiere Pro, I can adjust brightness, contrast, saturation, and color balance to bring out the desired effect. Whether I’m creating a warm, cinematic look or a cool, moody atmosphere, I use color grading to elevate the story and reinforce its emotional themes.

Software Expertise: DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, FilmConvert.

Motion Graphics

I have extensive experience in creating dynamic motion graphics and visual effects that add energy and creativity to videos. Using After Effects, I can create engaging animations, titles, and transitions that not only enhance the visual experience but also reinforce the message of the content. Whether it's an animated logo, lower thirds, or complex 3D effects, I bring creativity and precision to every frame.

Software Expertise: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Illustrator, Cinema 4D.

Sound Design & Mixing

Sound is just as important as visuals in creating a compelling story. I specialize in sound design and mixing, working to create immersive audio environments that match the tone of the visuals. I can clean up audio, create sound effects, and design custom soundscapes that add depth and emotion to a project. I also have experience mixing audio tracks for optimal clarity, ensuring that dialogue, music, and sound effects blend seamlessly to enhance the viewer's experience.

Software Expertise: Adobe Audition, Avid Pro Tools, iZotope RX.

My Projects

Corporate Video for NovaTech Solutions

I worked on a promotional video for NovaTech Solutions, a leading software company specializing in cutting-edge solutions for businesses. The project required me to capture the essence of their innovative approach and culture. Through cinematic storytelling, I highlighted the company's services, blending sleek visuals with dynamic transitions to showcase the products in action. I carefully crafted the pacing and narrative to align with the company's professional yet forward-thinking image, creating a powerful and engaging promotional video.

Tools Used: Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve.

YouTube Vlog Series for Alex Wanderlust

For Alex Wanderlust, a popular travel vlogger, I edited an exciting YouTube vlog series that took viewers to unique and off-the-beaten-path destinations. The footage was raw and unfiltered, requiring me to select and arrange the best shots to create an engaging and entertaining series. With the goal of showcasing both the beauty of the locations and Alex’s charismatic storytelling, I used dynamic editing techniques, including time-lapses, cutaways, and slow-motion, to keep the content fresh and immersive. Each episode was designed to capture the essence of the adventure, while maintaining a fun and energetic pace.

Tools Used: Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, After Effects.

Music Video for Skyline Echo

I collaborated with the indie band Skyline Echo to produce a high-energy music video for their hit single "Crimson Lights." The video required a mix of rhythmic cuts, glitch effects, and vibrant color grading to match the song's edgy and electric vibe. The project challenged me to push my creative boundaries by incorporating fast-paced transitions, bold color schemes, and visually striking effects that enhanced the intensity of the music. The final result was a visually captivating video that perfectly complemented the track’s dynamic energy.

Tools Used: Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve.
